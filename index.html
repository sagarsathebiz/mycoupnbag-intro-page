Powerful features

🛍️

One place for all coupons

Manual entry, forward-by-email, barcode scan, and Discover — all in one app.

📧

Forward email to add

Forward any promo email to your private address — we pull out the code and add it to your bag. Only you see the code.

✏️

Type it in or snap a pic

Got a code? Add it in seconds. Prefer a photo of the coupon? Attach it — we keep everything tidy with an optional “Shop now” link.

📷

Scan to save

Point your camera at a barcode or QR code — we fill in the code for you. Add details and save. Done.

🔍

Discover

Browse handpicked and community deals. Add to your bag and the code is yours — no hunting, no hassle.

🔔

Never miss an expiry

We’ll nudge you in the app (and optionally on your phone) when a coupon’s about to expire or when a new one lands in your bag.

How it works

✍️

The Manual Glow-up

Type it, tag it, save it. Pop in the code and merchant details to keep your savings organized and ready to deploy. Clean & Classic.

📧

The AI Inbox Hack

Stop copy-pasting. Forward any promo email to your private address, and our AI will strip the fine print to grab the code for you. Pure Magic.

🕵️‍♂️

The Deal Hunter

Don’t have a code? Find one. Browse the Marketplace for curated listings and community-sourced steals. Shop the World.
