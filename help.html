Help & FAQ

Frequently asked questions

  • no_sender: We couldn't identify the sender. Use your verified email to forward coupons.
  • unverified_sender: Email was from an address that isn't verified for your alias. Only you can add coupons from your email.
  • no_content: We couldn't find any coupon content in the email you forwarded.
  • no_coupon_code: We couldn't find a coupon code in the email. Make sure the email contains a clear code.
  • quota_exceeded: Your daily or monthly coupon limit was reached. / Email coupon limit reached for this month.
  • failed: Coupon from email could not be added. Try again or add it manually.

When a limit is hit you may see: "Coupon limit reached for this month", "Daily coupon limit reached", or "Email coupon limit reached for this month." Check your quota in the app (monthly total, daily non-manual, and email/month).
