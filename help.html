App Overview

MyCouponBag is your intelligent coupon management solution that helps you capture, organize, and use every coupon you come across before it expires!

🎯 What We Do

  • • AI-powered coupon import from images, documents, and text
  • • Smart organization and categorization
  • • Magic Search for finding relevant coupons
  • • Multi-platform access (mobile app, browser extension, bots)

💡 Key Benefits

  • • Never lose a coupon again
  • • Save time with automated processing
  • • Track your savings and usage
  • • Access exclusive partner offers
👜

MyBag Tab

This is your coupon collection! Here you can view, manage, and use all the coupons you've added to your account.

✨ Key Features

  • • View all your saved coupons in one place
  • • Filter coupons by category (Food, Shopping, Travel, etc.)
  • • Search for specific coupons using keywords
  • • Mark coupons as favorites for quick access
  • • See coupon details like expiry dates, discount amounts, and usage instructions
  • • Use the "Magic Search" feature to find coupons based on what you're looking for

🚀 How to Use

  • • Swipe through your coupons or use the search bar to find specific ones
  • • Tap on any coupon to see full details and copy the coupon code
  • • Use the category filters at the top to organize your coupons
  • • Try the Magic Search to discover relevant coupons for your needs
📋

Review Tab

This tab shows you coupons that have been automatically imported from your connected accounts (Email, Telegram etc) and need your review before being added to your collection.

✨ Key Features

  • • Review automatically imported coupons from your connected accounts
  • • Filter by source (Email, Telegram etc)
  • • Approve or reject imported coupons
  • • Edit coupon details before adding them to your collection
  • • See the original source of each coupon

🚀 How to Use

  • • Check this tab regularly for new imported coupons
  • • Review each coupon's details and accuracy
  • • Tap "Approve" to add it to your collection or "Reject" to remove it
  • • Edit any incorrect information before approving

Manual Entry Coupons

✨ Key Features

  • • Choose from predefined categories
  • • Set minimum payment requirements
  • • Add descriptions and notes
  • • Upload coupon images for reference

AI Submission Coupons

Let our AI help you add coupons quickly by processing text, images, or documents containing coupon information.

✨ Key Features

  • • Text Paste: Copy and paste coupon text from anywhere, and AI will extract all details
  • • Image Upload: Take a photo or upload an image of a coupon, and AI will read and extract information
  • • Document Upload: Upload PDF or document files containing coupon information
  • • Batch Processing: Process multiple images or documents at once
  • • Smart Extraction: AI automatically identifies brand, code, discount, expiry date, and other details

🚀 How to Use

  • • For Text: Copy coupon text from emails, websites, or messages, paste it in the clipboard section, and tap "Process"
  • • For Images: Tap the camera icon, take a photo or select from gallery, then tap "Process"
  • • For Documents: Tap the document icon, select your file, then tap "Process"
  • • Review the extracted information and make any corrections
  • • Tap "Add Coupon" to save to your collection
✨

Magic Search

An intelligent search feature that helps you find relevant coupons based on what you're looking for, even if you don't know the exact brand or coupon name.

✨ Key Features

  • • Search by product type, service, or general category
  • • Find coupons even without knowing specific brand names
  • • Get personalized coupon recommendations
  • • Search across all your saved coupons and available offers

🚀 How to Use

  • • Tap the Magic Search button
  • • Enter what you're looking for (e.g., "pizza delivery," "clothing store," "travel booking")
  • • Browse through relevant coupons and offers
  • • Tap on any result to see full details and use the coupon
