MyBag Tab

MyBag is your central coupon collection - think of it as your digital wallet where all your ready-to-use coupons live! This is where you'll find coupons that have been approved, processed, and are ready for immediate use.

✨ What You'll Find Here

  • • Approved Coupons: All coupons you've manually approved from the Review tab
  • • Direct Additions: Coupons you've added manually or via AI submission
  • • EliteLine Submissions: Premium coupons processed directly by our team
  • • Imported Offers: Coupons from the Discover tab that you've added to your collection
  • • Organized Categories: Food, Shopping, Travel, Entertainment, and more
  • • Usage Tracking: See which coupons you've used and your total savings

🚀 How to Use MyBag

  • • Browse: Swipe through your coupons or use the search bar for specific ones
  • • Filter: Use category filters (Food, Shopping, etc.) to find relevant coupons
  • • Magic Search: Try natural language search like "Show me food coupons under ₹500"
  • • Use Coupons: Tap any coupon to see full details and copy the code
  • • Favorites: Mark frequently used coupons as favorites for quick access
  • • Track Usage: Monitor which coupons you've used and your savings

💡 Pro Tips

  • • Check expiry dates regularly - expired coupons are automatically moved to a separate section
  • • Use Magic Search for complex queries like "coupons for pizza delivery near me"
  • • Favorite your most-used coupons for instant access
  • • Review your savings analytics to see how much you've saved over time

Review Tab

The Review tab is your quality control center! Here you'll find coupons that have been automatically imported from your connected accounts (Email, Telegram, WhatsApp, etc.) and need your approval before they're added to your MyBag collection.

✨ What Happens Here

  • • SmartBot Imports: Coupons sent via Telegram, WhatsApp, Email bots appear here
  • • AI Processing: Our AI extracts coupon details from your messages and images
  • • Manual Review: You verify the extracted information is accurate
  • • Source Tracking: See exactly where each coupon came from (Email, Telegram, etc.)
  • • Batch Actions: Approve or reject multiple coupons at once
  • • Edit Before Approval: Fix any incorrect details before adding to MyBag

🚀 Review Process

  • • Check Regularly: Review tab gets new coupons as you send them to bots
  • • Verify Details: Confirm brand name, coupon code, discount amount, expiry date
  • • Edit if Needed: Tap to edit any incorrect information
  • • Approve: Tap "Approve" to move coupon to MyBag
  • • Reject: Tap "Reject" to remove unwanted coupons
  • • Bulk Actions: Select multiple coupons for batch approval/rejection

🤖 SmartBot Workflow

  • • Send coupon images/text to our Telegram, WhatsApp, or Email bots
  • • AI automatically extracts coupon details and adds to Review tab
  • • You review and approve/reject each coupon
  • • Approved coupons move to MyBag for immediate use
  • • This ensures quality control while maintaining automation

✨ Submission Methods

🚀 How AI Submission Works

  • • For Text: Copy coupon text, paste in the app, tap "Process"

Magic Search

Magic Search is your intelligent coupon finder! Instead of searching for specific brand names or coupon codes, you can use natural language to find exactly what you need. It's like having a personal shopping assistant who knows all your coupons.

✨ How Magic Search Works

  • • Natural Language: Search using everyday language, not just keywords
  • • Smart Matching: AI understands context and intent behind your search
  • • Cross-Category Search: Find coupons across all categories at once
  • • Personalized Results: Results tailored to your usage patterns
  • • Real-time Updates: Searches include newly added coupons instantly
  • • Multi-Source Search: Searches both MyBag and Discover tab offers

🚀 Search Examples

Food & Dining

  • • "pizza delivery coupons"
  • • "food coupons under ₹500"
  • • "restaurant deals near me"
  • • "coffee shop discounts"

Shopping & Services

  • • "clothing store offers"
  • • "electronics discounts"
  • • "travel booking deals"
  • • "beauty salon coupons"

🎯 Advanced Search Tips

  • • Use Specific Terms: "pizza delivery" instead of just "food"
  • • Include Price Ranges: "coupons under ₹1000" or "deals above 50% off"
  • • Mention Timing: "expiring this week" or "new coupons"
  • • Specify Location: "near me" or "online only"
  • • Use Categories: "entertainment deals" or "shopping offers"
  • • Combine Terms: "pizza delivery under ₹500 expiring soon"

💡 Pro Tips

  • • Magic Search works best with descriptive, natural language
  • • The more specific your search, the better the results
  • • Use Magic Search before making any purchase to find relevant coupons
  • • Search results include both your saved coupons and available offers
  • • Try different variations if you don't get the results you want
  • • Magic Search learns from your usage to provide better results over time
