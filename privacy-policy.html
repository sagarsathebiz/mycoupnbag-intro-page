Privacy Policy for MyCouponBag

Last updated: August 2025

MyCouponBag ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information:

When you register or log in, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication details.

Purchase Information:

We use RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase tokens and transaction data from Google Play Billing to manage your subscription status.

Usage Data:

We may collect information about your interactions with the app, such as feature usage, clicks, and in-app activity.

Device Information:

Includes device model, operating system version, unique identifiers, and crash logs (collected via analytics or crash reporting tools, if applicable).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's features.
  • Manage and verify subscriptions and purchases via RevenueCat.
  • Improve user experience and app performance.
  • Communicate important updates or account-related information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide functionality:

  • RevenueCat (manages subscriptions and purchases).
  • Google Play Billing (processes payments).

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., RevenueCat, analytics providers) to operate the app.
  • Partner brands and retailers (only aggregated, anonymized coupon usage statistics to help improve deals and offers - no personal information is shared).
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Some information may be retained longer if required by law.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of certain data collection or marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on our website with an updated "Last updated" date.
