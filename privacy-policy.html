Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your information.

Overview

MyCouponBag ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

🛡️ Our Commitment

  • • We are committed to protecting your personal information
  • • We only collect information necessary to provide our services
  • • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • • We implement security measures to protect your data

📅 Last Updated

This policy was last updated in August 2025

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

👤 Account Information

  • • Name and email address
  • • Authentication details
  • • Profile preferences

💳 Purchase Information

  • • Subscription data via RevenueCat
  • • Transaction tokens
  • • Billing information

📱 Usage Data

  • • App interactions and features used
  • • Click patterns and preferences
  • • Performance analytics

🔧 Device Information

  • • Device model and OS version
  • • Unique identifiers
  • • Crash logs (if applicable)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

🎯 Primary Uses

  • • Provide and maintain app features and functionality
  • • Manage and verify subscriptions via RevenueCat
  • • Improve user experience and app performance
  • • Communicate important updates and account information
  • • Provide customer support and resolve issues

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide functionality:

💳 RevenueCat

  • • Manages subscriptions and purchases
  • • Processes payment tokens
  • • Handles billing verification

🛒 Google Play Billing

  • • Processes payments securely
  • • Manages transaction data
  • • Handles refunds and disputes

💬 Tawk.to Live Chat Support

  • • Provides real-time customer support within our app/website
  • • May collect chat content, device/browser details, IP, and info you share
  • • Used only to assist you and improve support; see Tawk.to Privacy Policy

ℹ️ Important Note

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them to understand how they handle your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

✅ When We Share Data

  • • Service Providers: RevenueCat, analytics providers to operate the app
  • • Partner Brands: Only aggregated, anonymized coupon usage statistics (no personal information)
  • • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

❌ What We Never Do

  • • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • • Share personal information with partner brands
  • • Use your data for unrelated purposes
⏰

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Some information may be retained longer if required by law.

📅 Retention Periods

  • • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • • Usage Data: Typically retained for 2 years
  • • Transaction Data: Retained as required by law
  • • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 1 year

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

🛡️ Security Measures

  • • Secure authentication and authorization

  • • Access controls and monitoring
  • • Compliance with industry standards
