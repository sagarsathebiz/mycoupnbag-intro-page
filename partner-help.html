For partners

MyCouponBag lets partners list deals on our marketplace and send curated coupons to specific users. End users see your offers in Discover and add them to their bag to reveal the code.

Partner Portal is in development right now. Meanwhile, if you want to launch deals for users, contact us at support@mycouponbag.com.

🤝

🎁 Launch offer (partners)

Limited-time benefits for partners who get in early — availability is based on entries and is first come, first served.

  • One marketplace coupon free — for a limited time, based on entries.
  • Publish banner ads for free — limited-time access to run in-app banner ads (image + redemption URL), based on entries.
  • Top visible placement — first come, first served for the most prominent slots while capacity lasts.

To claim or ask about eligibility, email support@mycouponbag.com.

🛒 Marketplace

Create coupons that appear in the Discover marketplace for all users. Users tap "Add to My Coupons" to add a copy to their bag (your code is shown only after they add). Partner accounts have access to create marketplace listings subject to plan limits (curated_per_day / curated_per_month).

🎯 Curated for you

Create curated coupons for a specific user (by user_id). They appear in that user's "Curated for you" section in Discover. The user adds the coupon to see the code. Same partner quota applies as for marketplace.

📣 Banner ads in app

Partners can place banner ads inside the app. Each ad can include an image plus a redemption URL (the link users open when they tap the banner).
